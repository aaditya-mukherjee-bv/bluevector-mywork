Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Agentic App Development and the SnapApp™ platform.

Can we use our existing SMS or Email providers?
expand_more

Yes. SnapApp is designed to be provider-agnostic. While we offer deep integration with Google Cloud's communication tools, we can also connect to your existing Twilio, SendGrid, or AWS SES accounts via secure APIs.

How do you handle language translation?
expand_more

Through our GenAI integration, SnapApp can automatically translate outbound messages into the recipient's preferred language based on their profile settings. This ensures your agency remains accessible to diverse communities without requiring manual translation for every alert.

What is a "Webhook" and why does my agency need it?
expand_more

A webhook is essentially a "digital doorbell." It allows an external system (like a third-party payment site) to ring SnapApp and say, "Hey, a payment was just made!" This allows SnapApp to instantly trigger the next step in your workflow, like sending a receipt or updating a permit status.

Is this secure enough for healthcare data?
expand_more

Absolutely. We support end-to-end encryption and specific "Secure Link" protocols. For sensitive healthcare information, instead of sending the data directly in an email or SMS, SnapApp can send a secure, one-time link that requires authentication to view.

Can we automate "Voice" calls for emergency alerts?
expand_more

Yes. Our platform supports automated outbound voice dialing. You can upload a list of contacts, and the system will use natural-sounding AI voices to read your message, ensuring that elderly constituents or those without internet access receive life-saving information.
